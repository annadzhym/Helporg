Highly Creative Solutions

Why we are Different

At MetaScience we support our members in their mission to advance research worldwide. Our hundreds of members based in over 15 countries around the world collectively publish journal articles, monographs, tutorials, and reference works.

We collaborate with scientists at every level of their careers. We believe it is crucial to present a balanced picture of the research community.

We are bringing the research of all kinds to the attention of a global audience of academia and the wider public.

We help researchers to conduct research more rigorously, and manage and share their work more openly.

From individuals to institutions, we consult and support research communities and their affiliates toward greater adoption of research and publication practices.
